Personal Information: When you register for an account, we collect your name and email address.

Usage Data: We may collect information about how you access and use our service, such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited.

2. How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect to:

Enable social media scheduling and content management.

Provide you with analytics and audit reports.

Communicate with you about your account and our service.

3. How We Share Your Information
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. Your data is used exclusively to provide the services you requested.

4. Data Security
We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Your data is stored on a secure server, and all communications are encrypted.

5. Your Rights
You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us.

6. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@socialsphere.com.
