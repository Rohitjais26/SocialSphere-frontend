User Data Deletion for SocialSphere
Effective Date: September 23, 2025

This page provides instructions on how to request the deletion of your personal data from SocialSphere.

1. How to Request Data Deletion
Email Request: To request that your personal data be deleted from our systems, please send an email to contact@socialsphere.com.

Required Information: In your email, please include your full name and the email address associated with your SocialSphere account. This will help us verify your identity and locate your data.

2. What Happens After Your Request
Upon receiving your request, we will process it within a reasonable timeframe.

We will notify you once your data has been successfully deleted from our systems.

Please note that some data may be retained for legal or operational purposes.

3. Deleting Social Media Connections
You can also disconnect your social media accounts at any time from your account settings. This will revoke our access to your social media data.
